about

Reliable by design

I build the systems behind streaming platforms — the APIs, subscription lifecycles, payment flows, and background pipelines that have to work every single time someone presses play.

Three things shape how I work: event-driven architecture (RabbitMQ workflows that keep services decoupled and fault-tolerant), observability (Prometheus metrics aggregated across clustered Node.js processes, visualized in Grafana — because you can't fix what you can't see), and performance engineering — caching, batching, and indexing applied where they actually move the numbers.

What I enjoy most is the intersection of building and firefighting: shipping a feature end to end, then when something breaks in production, tracing it to the root cause and fixing it permanently — not patching around it. Beyond code, I mentor interns and junior developers, lead code reviews, and collaborate closely with frontend, QA, and DevOps teams through every release.
